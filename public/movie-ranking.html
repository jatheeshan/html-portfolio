My Top 3 Movies

Amaran

This movie is about the life of a Indian military captain (Mukund Varadarajan) at the Kashmir border and how he manages family while being in the military

Oppenheimer

This movie is a biopic of someone who designed the atomic bomb that was later dropped in Hiroshima and Nagasaki during WW2

SpiderMan: Into the Spider Verse

This animated spiderman movie broke many barriers and had an amazing sound track that I still listen to. It was such a different experience seeing an animated spiderman. I believe it was well-executed and had an amazing storyline along with amazing graphics.
